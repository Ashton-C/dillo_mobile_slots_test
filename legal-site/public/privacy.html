Privacy Policy

Effective: 5/14/2026

This policy explains what data [LLC NAME] ("we") collects through our mobile games, why, and how to contact us.

Data we collect

  • Account identifier. When you launch the game we create an anonymous Firebase Auth account. If you set a display name or email, we store those too.
  • Game state. Resources, buildings, cosmetics, combat history, and similar gameplay data are stored in Firebase Firestore so your progress survives across sessions and devices.
  • Advertising ID + coarse location. Used by Google AdMob to serve ads. You can reset or opt out via your device's privacy settings.
  • Purchase history. If you make in-app purchases, RevenueCat receives the receipt to validate the transaction and unlock entitlements.
  • Crash & diagnostic data. Standard logs from Google Play / Firebase for stability — no identifying content.

What we don't collect

  • We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
  • We do not sell or rent personal data to third parties.
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, microphone, or precise location.

Third-party services

The game uses Firebase (Google), Google AdMob, RevenueCat, and Google Play Billing. Their own privacy policies apply to data they process: Firebase, AdMob, RevenueCat, Google.

Data retention & deletion

Game data persists as long as your account is active. To request deletion of your account and associated data, email [CONTACT EMAIL] from the address tied to the account (or include your in-game UID). We process requests within 30 days.
